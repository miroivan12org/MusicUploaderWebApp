1.Basic details
	Language
		language of titles, lyrics * 
	Release
		Title*
		Version (optional)
	Artist
		može biti compilation ako je, onda nema sljedećeg:
		Mora se navesti jedan po jedan artist
		Main * - upisati koji je main
		Mogu se dodati još iz padajućeg izbornika: Main, Featuring, Versus, Meets, Presents, With
	Genre
		MainGenre * odabire se između raznih žanrova
		Subgenre vezani padajući izbornik na MainGenre
	
2.Tracks
za svaku pjesmu potrebno je
	Track details 
		audio - waw*
		track title*
		version or remix name - dodatni text uz naslov (instrumental, mix)
		ISRC* - 12 characters, identify royalty payments
		Copyright year*
		Copyright holder recording* - year of first copyright and the name of the person/company who owns the copyright (can be artist name)
	Track artist
		name of the artists exactly as written on the cover*
		List each artist one by one: 
		Main* - upisati ga
		mogu se dodati još iz padajućeg izbornika: Main, Featuring, Versus, Meets, Presents, With
	Publishers
		Biraš koliko hoćeš publishera - company that looks after the "authors rights" - može i prazno
			ako je pjesma cover onda moraš naći originalnog izvođača
	Contributors
		Biraš rolu, contributora i publishera
		moraju biti composer i lyricist
		i još možeš dodati Arranger i Remixer
	Attributes
		Cover version yes/no
		Explicit content yes/no
		Live version yes/no
		Medley yes/no

	Copyright recording*
